This project investigates the impact of gender stereotype threats on academic outcomes. 
Using meta-analytic techniques, we synthesize data from multiple studies to quantify how stereotype threats influence academic performance and achievement.

**Collaborators**: Amor Ai, Sisley Yang, Frank Janicke, Eesha Nagpal, Camille Chandler, Phoebe Lam
