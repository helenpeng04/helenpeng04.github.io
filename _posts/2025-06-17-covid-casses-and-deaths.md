This project explores COVID-19 case and death patterns across Pennsylvania counties from April 2020 to December 2022. We analyzed trends over time, examined how population size relates to per-capita case and death rates, and investigated whether a higher number of cases corresponds to more deaths. We used exploratory data analysis (EDA), principal component analysis (PCA), and k-means clustering to uncover spatial and statistical patterns among counties. Clustering helped group counties with similar pandemic experiences based on standardized case and death rates.
