This project explores how gender stereotype threats affect memory performance, focusing on a heading recall task. By analyzing the data statistically, we investigate the cognitive biases introduced by stereotype threats and their impact on information retention. The goal is to understand how societal expectations can shape cognitive processes and influence memory recall.
